基础词 (Common),高级替换词 (Advanced Replacements),适用场景/微言大义
Important,"vital, crucial, prominent, cardinal, significant",cardinal 常用于原则；crucial 强调至关重要。
Good / Excellent,"outstanding, extraordinary, remarkable, eminent, top",extraordinary 强调非凡；eminent 常指人杰出。
Positive / Helpful,"beneficial, advantageous, conducive, favorable, rewarding",conducive 常接 to，表示对...有益。
Bad / Harmful,"detrimental, baneful, undesirable, adverse",detrimental 语气较重，指有害的。
Poor,"needy, impoverished, poverty-stricken, in poverty",impoverished 形容地区或家庭一贫如洗。
Rich,"wealthy, affluent, well-to-do, well-off, well-heeled",affluent 常形容社会或阶层富裕。
Big / Large,"vast, massive, enormous, tremendous, colossal",vast 常形容疆域或数字；massive 强调体量巨大。
Popular / Common,"prevailing, prevalent, pervasive, commonplace, universal",pervasive 强调无处不在的（多用于抽象事物）。
Interesting,"amusing, entertaining, engaging, thought-provoking",engaging 强调吸引人的；thought-provoking 发人深省。
Healthy,"robust, sound, wholesome",sound 常指建议或身体状态很“稳健”。
Famous,"renowned, noticeable, notable, celebrated",renowned 享誉盛名的。
Reasonable,"sound, rational, sensible",rational 强调理性、合乎逻辑。
Brave,"courageous, bold, fearless",bold 带有“大胆、闯劲”的意味。
Beautiful,"attractive, gorgeous, eye-catching, stunning",eye-catching 非常直观，指吸睛的。
New,"brand-new, fresh, novel",novel 侧重于“新颖的、前所未有的”。
